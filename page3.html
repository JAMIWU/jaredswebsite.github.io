• ⚬ • About Jared Wullschleger • ⚬ •

Jared Michael Wullschleger was born on February 9th, 2001 in North Platte Nebraska.
Since he was young, he loved creating in general. He used to doodle sketches on the back of assignment papers when he was bored. Years later, after he took Graphic Design classes in high school, he knew that was what he wanted to pursue as a career. Nowadays, he is taking a two-year course over at Mid-Plains Community College, in order to get a Graphic Design Degree.
